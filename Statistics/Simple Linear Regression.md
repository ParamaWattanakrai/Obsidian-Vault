In regression $x$ is the independent variable, and $y$ is the dependent variable.
$$
\begin{flalign}
\hat{y}=a+bx&&
\end{flalign}
$$

$$
\begin{flalign}
b=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^nx_iy_i-\frac{\sum_{i=1}^nx_i\sum_{i=1}^ny_i}n}{\sum_{i=1}^nx_i^2-\frac{(\sum_{i=1}^nx_i)^2}{n}}&&
\end{flalign}
$$
$$
\begin{flalign}
a=\bar{y}-b\bar{x}&&
\end{flalign}
$$
